About

Hi ! I am Foysal Khandakar Joy, completed Bachelor or Science in Robotics and Mechatronics Engineering from University of Dhaka, Bangladesh.

During my undergraduate I always tried to learn some valuable skills that can speak for me when my academic result can't speak showing that much confidence. The world is becoming more digitized so are the problems too. So, I fell in love with problem-solving through programming where I can solve thousands of problems with one automated solution. I learned many data structures and algorithms for that purpose. And the result is I participated in ICPC Dhaka Regional 2019 Asia site. Now, the time has come that solution can be created from the problem itself and here come the machine learning, data science field with statistical theory. I again fell in love to make a program where the program can learn itself by fitting curve to previously seen events. It seems more interesting. So, I started learning the things and practicing them online platform like kaggle. And recently I finished on top 15\% in Google Quest Challenge held in kaggle. My final year project was ``Robot Navigation from Natural Language Instructions" where the model could predict the actual sequence of goal from the given random instructions. It was more interesting and the same approach I used in Google Quest Challenge with different architecture. So these things are always interesting to me.
