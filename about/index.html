About

Hi ! I am Foysal Khandakar Joy, completed Bachelor or Science in Robotics and Mechatronics Engineering from University of Dhaka, Bangladesh.

During my undergraduate I always tried to learn some valuable skills with fun that can speak for me when my academic result can't speak with that much confidence. I fell in love with problem-solving through programming where I could solve thousands of problems with one automated solution. Then I started practicing sport programming problems. I learned some data structures and algorithms for that purpose. My team DU_SwanSong qualified for ICPC Asia Regional Dhaka site 2019 by competing with 1805 teams from all over the country.

Which are complex to automate through explicit instructions can be solved through implicit where the solution can be created from the problem itself and here is the the machine learning, data science field with statistical theory. It seemed more interesting. So, I started learning the things and practicing them on platform like kaggle. Recently I finished on top 15\% in Google Quest Challenge held in kaggle. My final year project was ``Robot Navigation from Natural Language Instructions" where the model could predict the actual sequence of goal from the given random instructions. Still there are manythings to learn in this area.
